WHO WE ARE

About Silent Coworking Space

Silent Coworking Space was established with a single purpose — to provide professionals in Mota Varachha, Surat with a dedicated, productive, and affordable workspace.

We understand that working from home or a noisy café can hinder your focus and growth. That is why we created a space that is built for serious work.

Located at C2-817, Pragati IT Park, Opp. A.R. Mall, Mota Varachha, Surat, we are conveniently accessible for professionals across Varachha, Kapodra, Katargam, and nearby areas. Our facility is designed to support individuals and teams who value silence, professionalism, and productivity.

At Silent Coworking Space, we are not just offering a desk — we are building a community of driven professionals who work, connect, and grow together.

Our Mission

To empower professionals with a world-class workspace environment that encourages focus, collaboration, and business growth — right here in Surat.
